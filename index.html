Fairness Track at TREC 2019

TREC 2019 will feature a track on fairness in information retrieval. The Fair Ranking track focuses on building two-sided systems that offer fair exposure to ranked content producers while ensuring high results quality for ranking consumers.

Watch this space for more details; data and metrics will be coming in May 2019.
